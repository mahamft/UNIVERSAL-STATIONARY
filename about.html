The Universal Story 📖

From Local Pamphlets to Digital Excellence.

Universal Stationary has been a trusted name for years. Initially, we reached our customers through pamphlets and TV advertisements. However, with the rapid development of the internet, we have launched this digital portal to bring our complete catalog to your fingertips.

We take pride in offering premium brands like STAPLES, Parker, and Faber-Castell. Our mission is to provide high-quality stationery that fuels creativity in schools, offices, and art studios across Pakistan.
